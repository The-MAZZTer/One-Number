Known Bugs

Most of the service error-related bugs seem to be caused by the services or Chrome and I have been unable to work around them. If you know how to work around any of these please contact me.

  • When logging into Google, you need to visit Google Voice and Google Wave before either of those services can be checked using this extension. If you keep logged on between browser sessions this shouldn't be more than a minor annoyance.
  • Log off doesn't always work for Google Wave. You may need to try again or visit the Google Wave service and use that log off link.
  • When using explicit username/password in GMail, GMail will keep you logged in as that user.
  • If you have the GPU Accelerated 2d Canvas experiment enabled, One Number will hang when attempting to show its popup. You can track this bug here.
  • Toolbar button images and notification images are not properly anti-aliased and so don't look as good as they could. You can star this bug to show your request here.
  • Google Wave seems to no longer respect the contact pane minimize preference.
  • Various settings.js errors in console (need stack traces plz!).
  • Gmail sometimes shows all unread items even when you didn't have that option selected (check Options and try selecting "Inbox" instead of the other options). Let me know if "Priority Inbox" works as expected or not, too, thx.
  • When recycling tabs, if the tab(s) are in another window that window will not be brought to the foreground.
  • Some people don't like the new icon. Icon selection will be coming in a future version.
  • Some people don't like that One Number now ignores your Google Reader Home page option. A toggle will be added to restore old behavior.
